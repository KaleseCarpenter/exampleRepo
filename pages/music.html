I love music and I will ultimately work in the music industry as a Software Engineer and a business owner. I feel like there is a huge void in the music industry that needs to be filled and independent artists need more help. I go to a lot of underground hip hop shows, concerts and events in the community. I love to make music myself but I found out early in the business that I would rather work behind the scenes. I will change the game in my own creative way and I believe that whole heartedly. I am not happy with the current state of mainstream hip-hop. I feel like alot of the stuff being pushed to the masses is doing nothing for the youth mentally and spiritually. I believe that there should be an even balance with more positive music. Lately I have been listening to more R&B and early 2000's rock. Some of my favorite artists right now are Dayo Gold, Joey Bada$$, Tyler the Creator, Thundercat, Alex Isley, Moonchild, and Ari Lennox.




My Top 4 Rappers




Favorite Rapper Favorite Album Favorite Hip-Hop Moment
Lil Wayne Carter II & Carter III In 2008 when he was killing every single song he was on. He took over rap and sold 1 million records 1st week.
Jay Z The Blueprint It was like a cultural reset when he dropped Death of Auto-Tune. Many rappers began using auto-tune heavy. People were copying Lil Wayne and T-Pain alot so it was very refreshing for him to bring it back to the roots of hiphop in his own unique way.
The Notorious B.I.G. Life After Death When he said "Black and ugly as ever, however I stay Coogi down to the socks." I personally believe that we should all speak highly of ourselves but that bar was super hard!
Wiz Khalifa Taylor Allderdice When he started releasing DayToday vlogs showing his creative process and life on tour. Those videos motivated me so much during a bad period in my life and motivated me to start vlogging my life as well.
